Get your service URL:
  minikube service {{ .Release.Name }}-service -n {{ .Values.namespace }} --url

Test your API:
  curl http://YOUR_URL/health
  curl http://YOUR_URL/

Generate AI test cases:
  curl -X POST http://YOUR_URL/generate-tests \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{"endpoint": "/login", "method": "POST", "description": "Login API", "num_cases": 3}'

Check pods:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.namespace }}
